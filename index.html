    Calculation Model

    Parallel group: G = C1 + C2 + C3 + C4. Arm capacitance: Carm = 1 / (1/G1 + ... + 1/G6). With relay treated as an approximate short, top arms A/B are in parallel and bottom arms C/D are in parallel. The displayed unbalanced current is |IA - IC|, equivalent to wV |CA*CD - CB*CC| / (CA + CB + CC + CD).
