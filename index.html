    Calculation Details

    Calculation Model

    Parallel group: G = C1 + C2 + C3 + C4. Arm capacitance: Carm = 1 / (1/G1 + ... + 1/G6). With relay treated as an approximate short, top arms C1/C3 are in parallel and bottom arms C2/C4 are in parallel. The displayed unbalanced current is |IA - IC|, equivalent to wV |C1*C4 - C3*C2| / (C1 + C2 + C3 + C4).
